# User Guide

This guide explains how to use the various tools available in AIO Utilities.

## 🎂 Age Calculator
*   **Age from Birthday**: Enter your birth date to see your age in years, months, and days.
*   **Age Gap**: Select two different dates to calculate the exact time difference between them.

## 📏 Unit Converter
Choose a category (Length, Mass, Temperature, etc.), enter a value, and see instant conversions across all common units. 
*   *Tip*: Units are automatically sorted by magnitude for easier reading!

## 🌍 World Clock & Timezone Converter
*   **Watchlist**: Search for any city or country and click "Add" to keep it in your permanent watchlist.
*   **Time Conversion**: Use the interactive timeline to see what time it will be in another city at a specific moment.
*   **Day/Night indicators**: Icons help you see at a glance if it's currently business hours in a target location.

## 📝 Grammar Checker
Paste your text into the editor and click **Check**.
*   **Highlights**: Hover over highlighted text to see the grammar rule.
*   **Suggestions**: Click a word to see and apply corrections.
*   **Fix All**: Use the "Auto-Fix All" button for one-click editing.

## 🔓 PDF Password Remover
Drag and drop your protected PDF. Enter the password once, and the tool will generate a decrypted version for download. 
*   **Privacy**: This process is 100% client-side; your PDF is never uploaded.

## 🖼️ Image Tools
*   **Format Converter**: Convert between PNG, JPG, WEBP, and SVG. Adjust the quality slider to see real-time file size estimates.
*   **Compressor**: Set a target file size (e.g., 200KB), and the tool will automatically scale and compress the image to match your limit.

## 🔗 QR Code Generator
Enter a URL or text to generate a code.
*   **Customization**: Change background/foreground colors.
*   **Logos**: Upload a logo and position it anywhere on the QR code (center or corners). High error correction (Level H) is applied automatically for safety.
